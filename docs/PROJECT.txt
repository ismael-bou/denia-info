================================================================
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================
denia.info is a hyperlocal digital platform connecting tourists 
and residents of Denia, Spain with the best restaurants, bars, 
hotels, events, and activities the city has to offer.

Unlike generic platforms such as TripAdvisor or TheFork, 
denia.info is built from the ground up for one city, by someone 
who lives there. Every listing is verified in person. Every menu 
is real. Every translation is quality-checked.

The platform is powered by Claude AI (Anthropic) for intelligent 
features including multilingual support, smart search, automated 
menu descriptions, and a tourist chatbot — making denia.info the 
most technologically advanced local platform on the Costa Blanca.

Target market:
- 500,000+ tourists who visit Denia annually
- 45,000 local residents
- 300+ restaurants, bars, and hotels in Denia


================================================================
PROBLEM WE SOLVE
================================================================
Tourists arriving in Denia face a fragmented experience:

- Google Maps shows basic info but no full menus
- TripAdvisor has reviews but no real-time booking
- TheFork covers big chains but ignores small local gems
- Local websites are outdated, not mobile-friendly, not multilingual
- No single platform covers restaurants + bars + hotels + events

Restaurant owners face the same problem from the other side:
- No affordable way to showcase their full menu online
- No simple booking system for small businesses
- No multilingual presence to reach European tourists
- Dependent on expensive platforms that take high commissions

denia.info solves both sides of this problem.


================================================================
SOLUTION
================================================================
A fast, mobile-first, AI-powered platform that gives tourists 
everything they need to discover and book the best of Denia — 
and gives restaurant owners a free, powerful tool to attract 
more customers.

Core pillars:
1. COMPLETE INFORMATION  — Full menus, real photos, accurate hours
2. EASY BOOKING          — Table reservations in 3 clicks
3. MULTILINGUAL          — ES, EN, FR, DE, NL (AI-powered)
4. LOCAL INTELLIGENCE    — AI chatbot trained on Denia data
5. TRUSTED              — Every listing verified in person

================================================================
AI FEATURES (Powered by Claude API)
================================================================
1. TOURIST CHATBOT
   Natural language assistant on the homepage.
   Tourists ask: "Where can I eat paella near the beach tonight?"
   Claude answers based on real listings and availability.

2. MENU AUTO-TRANSLATION
   Upload a menu in Spanish — Claude translates it instantly
   into EN, FR, DE, NL with natural, accurate language.

3. MENU DESCRIPTION GENERATOR
   Upload a photo of a dish — Claude writes a professional,
   appetizing description automatically.

4. REVIEW SUMMARIZER
   Claude reads all reviews for a restaurant and generates
   a short, honest summary for tourists at a glance.

5. SEO CONTENT GENERATOR
   Claude auto-generates optimized page descriptions for 
   every listing in all 5 languages.

6. DAILY SOCIAL MEDIA POSTS
   Claude generates daily LinkedIn and Instagram content
   featuring Denia restaurants and food culture.

7. OWNER MORNING BRIEFING
   Daily email to platform owner: new bookings, new reviews,
   site stats, and action items for the day.

DATABASE STRUCTURE
================================================================
restaurants
  id, name, slug, address, area, lat, lng, 
  phone, email, website, cuisine_type, price_range,
  opening_hours, is_featured, is_verified, created_at

menus
  id, restaurant_id, category, item_name, 
  description_es, description_en, description_fr, 
  description_de, description_nl, price, photo_url

bookings
  id, restaurant_id, user_name, user_email, 
  user_phone, date, time, guests, status, 
  confirmation_code, created_at

reviews
  id, restaurant_id, user_name, rating, comment, 
  language, source, created_at

users
  id, email, preferred_language, nationality, 
  loyalty_points, created_at

events
  id, title, description, date, time, 
  location, category, is_free, price, created_at
