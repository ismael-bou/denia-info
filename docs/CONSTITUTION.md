# denia.info — Platform Constitution
> The principles, rules, and standards that govern how denia.info 
> is built, operated, and grown.

**Owner:** Ismael Boukhouit  
**Domain:** denia.info  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 2026  
**Status:** Active  

---

## PREAMBLE

denia.info exists to serve two communities simultaneously:
the tourists who visit Denia and the local businesses that 
welcome them. Every decision made on this platform — technical, 
commercial, or editorial — must serve both communities fairly 
and honestly.

This constitution is the single source of truth for how 
denia.info operates. It does not change based on commercial 
pressure, shortcuts, or convenience.

---

## PART I — MISSION & VISION

### Mission
To be the most trusted, accurate, and useful digital platform 
for discovering and experiencing Denia, Spain.

### Vision
A world where every tourist who arrives in Denia finds exactly 
what they are looking for — and every local business gets the 
visibility it deserves — without paying a fortune to big 
platforms that do not understand the city.

### Core Promise
- To tourists:    "You will always find accurate, up-to-date 
                   information on denia.info."
- To businesses:  "We will represent you honestly and help you 
                   reach more customers."
- To Denia:       "We will promote this city with pride and 
                   respect for its culture and community."

---

## PART II — CORE VALUES

### 1. LOCAL FIRST
Every decision prioritizes the Denia community over growth 
metrics, revenue, or outside interests. We are a local platform 
built by a local person. That is our identity and our advantage.

### 2. ACCURACY OVER SPEED
A listing with wrong opening hours or an outdated menu damages 
trust more than having no listing at all. We verify before 
we publish. We update before we promote.

### 3. RADICAL TRANSPARENCY
- We tell restaurant owners exactly what we do with their data
- We tell tourists exactly how listings are ranked
- We never accept payment to fake reviews or rankings
- Featured listings are always clearly labeled as such

### 4. QUALITY OVER QUANTITY
30 perfect listings beat 300 incomplete ones. Every listing 
on denia.info must meet the minimum quality standard before 
it goes live.

### 5. TECHNOLOGY SERVES PEOPLE
AI and automation are tools, not replacements for human 
judgment. Every AI-generated translation, description, or 
recommendation is reviewed before it is published.

### 6. RESPECT FOR CULTURE
Denia has a rich Spanish and Valencian identity. The platform 
celebrates this culture — it does not dilute it for the sake 
of international appeal.

## PART III — LISTING STANDARDS

### Minimum Requirements for a Live Listing
Every restaurant, bar, or hotel listed on denia.info MUST have:

- [ ] Verified name and address
- [ ] At least one real photo (not stock photography)
- [ ] Accurate opening hours
- [ ] Phone number or booking contact
- [ ] At least one menu category with real items and prices
- [ ] Cuisine type and price range selected
- [ ] Owner consent to be listed

### Quality Standards
- Photos must be real, recent, and well-lit
- Menu prices must be current (updated at least every 6 months)
- Opening hours must reflect current reality
- Descriptions must be honest — no exaggeration

### Prohibited Content
- Stock photos presented as real restaurant photos
- Fake reviews of any kind
- Misleading pricing or hidden fees
- Content that discriminates against any group
- Listings added without owner knowledge or consent

### Listing Removal Policy
A listing will be removed if:
- The business has permanently closed
- The owner formally requests removal
- The listing repeatedly fails quality standards
- The business violates the prohibited content policy

---

## PART IV — REVIEW POLICY

### Authentic Reviews Only
- Reviews must come from real visitors with a real experience
- No anonymous reviews without verified email
- No reviews from business owners about their own business
- No incentivized reviews ("leave a review and get 10% off")

### Review Moderation
- All reviews are read before being published
- Reviews are removed if they contain hate speech, 
  personal attacks, or clearly false information
- Negative reviews are NOT removed simply because 
  the business owner requests it
- If a business disputes a review, both sides are heard

### AI Review Summaries
- Claude AI summarizes reviews for quick reading
- Summaries are clearly labeled as AI-generated
- The original reviews are always visible and accessible

---

## PART V — RANKING & FEATURING

### How Listings Are Ranked (Default)
Default ranking is based on:
1. Completeness of listing (full menu, photos, hours)
2. Review rating and volume
3. Recency of last update
4. Proximity to user location (when enabled)

### Featured Listings
- Businesses can pay to appear in the Featured section
- Featured listings are always clearly labeled "Featured"
- Payment NEVER affects review scores or editorial content
- A featured listing with bad reviews still shows bad reviews

### What Money Cannot Buy
- Removal of negative reviews
- Higher star ratings
- Editorial recommendations ("Ismael's Picks")
- Suppression of competitor listings

---

## PART VI — DATA & PRIVACY

### User Data
- We collect only what is necessary to provide the service
- Booking data is used only to facilitate the booking
- We never sell user data to third parties
- Users can request deletion of their data at any time

### Restaurant Owner Data
- Business data is used only to power their listing
- We never share owner contact details publicly without consent
- Owners can update or delete their listing at any time

### AI & Data
- Claude AI processes listing data to generate translations 
  and descriptions
- No personal user data is sent to the AI
- AI-generated content is always reviewed before publishing

### Cookies
- We use only essential cookies for the platform to function
- Analytics cookies require explicit user consent
- We never use tracking cookies for advertising

---

## PART VII — COMMERCIAL PRINCIPLES

### Pricing Philosophy
- The basic listing is free forever for Denia businesses
- Premium features must deliver clear, measurable value
- We never raise prices on existing customers without 
  90 days notice
- Small family-run businesses get the same quality 
  of service as large hotels

### Commission Policy
- Booking commissions are fixed, small, and transparent
- Commission rates are published publicly on the website
- We never change commission rates without prior notice

### Advertiser Independence
- Advertisers have no influence over editorial content
- No business can pay to have a competitor delisted
- Sponsored content is always clearly labeled

---

## PART VIII — PLATFORM GROWTH PRINCIPLES

### Expand Only When Ready
We do not expand to new cities (Javea, Calpe, Altea) until 
denia.info is fully stable, profitable, and the Denia 
community is genuinely well served.

Growth for the sake of growth is not a goal.

### Quality Gates Before Expansion
Before expanding to any new city:
- [ ] Denia has 100+ verified listings
- [ ] Platform is profitable in Denia
- [ ] A local contact exists in the new city
- [ ] The tech is stable with zero critical bugs

### Community Before Commerce
We build community trust before we monetize. No paywall 
or premium feature is introduced before the platform 
has proven its value for free.

---

## PART IX — TECHNOLOGY PRINCIPLES

### Performance Standards
- Homepage must load in under 2 seconds on mobile
- The platform must work on slow 3G connections
- Every page must be fully accessible on any screen size

### AI Usage Standards
- AI assists humans — it does not replace human judgment
- Every AI-generated text is reviewed before publishing
- AI errors are corrected immediately when found
- Users are informed when content is AI-generated

### Security Standards
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- No passwords stored in plain text — ever
- Security vulnerabilities are fixed within 24 hours
- Regular backups of all platform data

### Open by Default
- Code is open source on GitHub
- Platform decisions are documented publicly
- This constitution is publicly available in the repo

---

## PART X — AMENDMENTS

This constitution can be updated when:
1. The platform evolves beyond its original scope
2. New legal requirements apply
3. Community feedback reveals a gap or error

Every amendment must:
- Be documented with a version number and date
- Explain what changed and why
- Be committed to the GitHub repository
